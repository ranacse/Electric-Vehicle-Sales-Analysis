# Electric Vehicle (EV) Sales Intelligence Dashboard
### Tools: Power BI | SQL | Data Modeling

## Project Overview
I developed this global interactive dashboard to analyze the performance and market trends of Electric Vehicles across regional markets. Analyzing a massive dataset of **6 million units**, this project provides strategic insights into sales growth, regional distribution, and category-specific market share (BEV vs. PHEV).

## Key Insights & Metrics
* **Market Scale**: Visualized a total sales volume of 6.00M units.
* **Monthly Growth**: Analyzed a 2.44% monthly growth rate to identify seasonal trends.
* **Regional Performance**: Identified the top-performing regions and states, tracking the distribution of EV adoption.
* **Category Breakdown**: Compared Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV) to gauge consumer preferences.
* **Technological Trends**: Tracked average Electric Range (6.8k) across the fleet.

## Technical Skills Applied
* **SQL**: Used for data preparation, cleaning, and structuring the 6M records for efficient reporting.
* **Data Modeling**: Established relationships between regional and sales tables to enable seamless filtering.
* **Visualization**: Designed high-density charts including Tree Maps, Gauges, and Time-series Area Charts for complex data storytelling.
